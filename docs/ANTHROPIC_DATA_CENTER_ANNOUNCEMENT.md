## üìã Key Commitments

### 1. Cover Infrastructure Costs
- Pay for 100% of grid infrastructure upgrades (transmission lines, substations)
- These costs will NOT be passed on to consumers
- Anthropic will absorb costs via increased monthly electricity charges

### 2. Procure New Power
- Bring net-new power generation online to match data center needs
- Where new generation isn't online yet, work with utilities to estimate and cover demand-driven price effects

### 3. Reduce Grid Strain
- Invest in curtailment systems (cut power usage during peak demand)
- Deploy grid optimization tools
- Both keep prices lower for ratepayers

### 4. Invest in Local Communities
- Create hundreds of permanent jobs + thousands of construction jobs
- Deploy water-efficient cooling technologies
- Partner with local leaders on AI benefit-sharing initiatives
